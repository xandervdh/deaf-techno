About Deaf Techno

Deaf Techno is a techno festival held yearly in Belgium.
Our goal is to bring the best of techno, both famous and fresh talent, to our listeners.
We do our best to keep our prices manageable.

Deaf Techno is a summer festival held from July 1th-4th, there is an option to camp, you can find more information under practical. There will be separate tickets for each day or a combination ticket for those that prefer the full experience of Deaf Techno.
